Product Group Information

Each Product Group has a few fields that need to be completed. The first is the name that will be displayed for the product group. The second is an image for the product group thumbnail. The third is a time in seconds on the timeline of the video where the product group is to start. For example, the first product group should be default start at time 0. No two product groups should have the same time. The video preview can be used to find the best timing.

Product Group Products

Click the Add Product button for every product in the Product Group.
