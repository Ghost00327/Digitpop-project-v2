Product Groups

Product Groups are groups of products that have an association with the video timeline. For example, a fashion show that has ten models walking in one at a time has ten product groups. Each model displays a group of products during a time period. Model one may be displayed from the beginning to ten seconds in. Model two then would appear from ten seconds to twenty seconds, and so on for each successive model.

Product Group in Video

The products in a product group are displayed when the viewer taps on the screen. In this example, a product group is displayed for blush and bronzer in a cosmetics tutorial.
