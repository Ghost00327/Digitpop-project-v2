Upload Video

Upload a video that showcases your products. Shoppable Video is capable of showcasing multiple products at a time. Take the classic fashion show format for example. Each model has a look that is composed of multiple products working in concert. It is suggested that all the products are displayed for enough time for the viewer to properly evaluate the product. A steady rhythm, for example of five to ten seconds per product group is suggested. Any editing of the video should be done before this step.
