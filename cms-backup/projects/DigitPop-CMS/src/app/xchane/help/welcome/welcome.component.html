Welcome

Welcome to DigitPop! This is a quick introduction that will show how to earn access to your favorite online media.

Dashboard

The Dashboard is where you launch the shoppable video ads to earn points for gift cards for your favorite Video, Music and News. The media provider that you're earning points towards is selected in the rewards section.

Points

This is where your point score is. You use the points to earn access to the media providers. The Points are what you earn for completing shoppable video ads. We will show you where to launch the video ads next.

Videos and Categories

This is where you launch the video ads to earn points. Watch the video the full way through and answer the question at the end of the video corrrectly in order to receive points. Select a category that you are interested in to launch a video. When you have enough points then you can redeem them for access to media in the next step.

Project Wizard

This is where you redeem points for rewards. Select a reward that you have enough points for and an electronic gift card will be sent to your email.
